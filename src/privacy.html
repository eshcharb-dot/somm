Privacy & Terms

Somm — a personal AI wine companion.

Legal drinking age. Somm recommends alcoholic beverages. It is intended only for people of legal drinking age in their country/region. By using the app you confirm you meet that requirement. Somm does not sell or serve alcohol — it only provides information and recommendations.

What we collect

  • On-device: your taste profile, ratings, budget preferences and chat history are stored in your browser's local storage by default — no account required.
  • If you sign in: the same data (chat history, ratings, taste profile) is also stored in our cloud database (Supabase), tied to your account, so it can sync across devices. Access is restricted to your account via row-level security.
  • Photos: when you scan a bottle, shelf, wine list or menu, the photo is sent to our backend and forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Photos are never stored on our servers; your recent scans (including the photo) are kept on your device only, and are removed by "Reset everything". Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to that processing.
  • Chat messages: sent to our backend, which proxies them to Claude (or Groq) to generate Vera's replies. Backend request logs may briefly contain message text for debugging/abuse-prevention purposes.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.
  • We don't require an account to use the core app — signing in is optional and only adds cross-device sync.

Your controls

  • Export your profile as JSON any time from the You tab.
  • Reset everything stored locally on this device from the You tab.
  • Delete your cloud data permanently (chat history, ratings, taste profile) from the You tab if you're signed in. This does not delete your sign-in account itself.

Terms

Somm's recommendations are informational, not professional advice. Price and market estimates come from the AI's training knowledge, not a live price feed, and may be inaccurate — always verify before buying. Drink responsibly and in accordance with the laws where you live.
